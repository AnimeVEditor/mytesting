Our Story

Accessible Luxury for the Whole Family

Nestled on BT Road in Kolkata’s Dunlop area, The Spice Route is a premium multi-cuisine restaurant blending rich culinary traditions. Our chefs craft flavorful, authentic meals in a warm, inviting atmosphere decorated with deep saffron and burgundy accents.

Whether it's a family dinner, a celebration, or a cozy evening out, we promise fast service, fresh quality, and an unforgettable taste at a moderate price.
